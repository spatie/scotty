---
title: Pause and resume
weight: 1
---

Press `p` any time during execution. Scotty immediately acknowledges the pause request in the status line and pauses between the current and next task.

![Pause and resume](https://github.com/spatie/scotty/blob/main/docs/images/scotty-pause.jpg?raw=true)

Press `Enter` to resume, or `Ctrl+C` to cancel.

## Cancelling

Press `Ctrl+C` to cancel at any time. Scotty restores the terminal and exits cleanly. All output stays in your scrollback.
